📕 Big zine (saddle-stitched) — how to make it

1. Print → A5, double-sided, and in the dialog pick "Booklet" layout if available (it reorders pages for you). Turn ON background graphics.
2. Fold the whole stack in half. Staple twice on the fold = saddle stitch. (Tuto in the README.)
3. The VECTOR slot = your 1 vector drawing from the first classes. The RASTER ×10 slots = 10 images from your second project. Print/glue or drop image files in.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Genuary is a worldwide challenge: every day of January you code one piece of generative art from a given prompt.

I built a website that runs all 31 sketches live in the browser with p5.js, plus a non-AI chatbot (pure keyword rules, no GPT) that explains the code.

This saddle-stitched zine collects the highlights on paper: the art, a QR to the live site, and how it was made.
